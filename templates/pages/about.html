Our Mission

Our Story

How Clearview came to be

The Idea

It started with a simple question: "What does 75°F and sunny really look like?" Weather apps show numbers, but they don't show you the experience. We wanted to change that.

Building the Platform

We combined real-time weather data with user-generated content to create something unique—a weather app that shows you actual moments from around the world.

Adding Atmosphere

Weather has a soundtrack. We integrated ambient music that matches current conditions, creating an immersive, multi-sensory experience.

Global Community

Today, thousands of users share their weather moments daily, creating a living gallery of Earth's atmospheric beauty.
